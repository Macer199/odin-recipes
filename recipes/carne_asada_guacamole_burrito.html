Carne Asada Guacamole Burrito

Description

A delicious Mexican staple, the burrito is a food that is so popular within the American food culture that there has been debate as to whether it is Mexican or actually American. The fact of the matter is, it's Mexican. But that should not stop you from enjoying the flavors that it offers. Wrapped within a tortilla, the combination of meat, cheese, sour cream, and guacamole will provide a wonderful experience.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Carna asada (flank steak)
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 limes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 small stalks of cilantro
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • Cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of sour cream

Steps

  1. Season your carne asada with salt and pepper, and place in a pan with a light drizzle of olive oil. The steak should be at least medium rare. Flip when needed.
  2. Cut your avocado, cilantro and lime, and dice your tomato and onion.
  3. Scoop the avocado into a bowl with your diced tomato and onion, squeeze some lime, put a dash of salt and pepper, and squish and mix really well after putting in the cilantro. Add more lime, salt, pepper, and cilantro to taste.
  4. Grate 4 ounces of cheddar cheese on a plate.
  5. When your carne asada is done cooking, place on a plate and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  6. While your carne asada is resting, lay out a flour tortilla, spread the sour cream and place some grated cheese on it.
  7. Once the carne asada rested, cut into 1-inch strips, and put it on the tortilla, as well as your guacamole.
  8. Fold the tortilla into a burrito and cook it for a few minutes more on a pan to warm it up.
  9. Slice it in half and serve on a plate.
  10. Enjoy!
