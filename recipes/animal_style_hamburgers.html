Animal Style Hamburgers

Description

If you are a Californian, it is very likely that you have heard of In-N-Out, an iconic hamburger staple with a simple, yet never-changing menu of burgers, fries, and shakes. Priding itself on making things the old-fashioned way, quality is the most important ingredient. A close second would be their animal style sauce, which can elevate your experience and provide new flavor to the burgers and fries they serve. Now you can make it home, and experience the same deliciousness you would in In-N-Out. Here, we will make two single patty, animal style hamburgers.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • 1 stalk of lettuce
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • White vinegar
  • Sweet pickle relish
  • American cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast four buns on a pan and remove when golden brown.
  2. Mix your ground beef with salt and pepper, form two patties, and place on the same pan.
  3. As the patties are cooking, take 3 tablespoons of ketchup, 1/2 cup of mayonnaise, 1 teaspoon each of mustard and white vinegar, and 2 1/2 tablespoons of sweet pickle reslish, and mix in a bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste. This would be your animal style sauce.
  4. When the patties are cooked on one side, flip to cook the other side, and place a slice of american cheese on each patty.
  5. While the patties are cooking, cut some lettuce and 4 slices each of your tomato and onion.
  6. Just before your patties are cooked, spread the sauce on each bun.
  7. On a plate, stack your ingredients for each burger in the following order - bottom bun, patty with cheese, 2 onion slices, 2 tomato slices, lettuce, top bun.
  8. Enjoy!
